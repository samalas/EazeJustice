The Future

How Blockchain can help improve the system!

Traffic Law, Personal Injury Law, Criminal Defense Law

There is very little to almost no transparency between the client, lawyer, and the court. When a client gets a claim or fine, this is processed and it takes weeks to be verified and notified to the court and client. The client then tries to fight it by calling a lawyer, who then proceeds to state he will take the information about the fine/claim and proceed with legal action. The client then pays a full amount fee for the lawyerâ€™s services while blindly trusting his word. While this is all happening, the client has no real proof if the lawyer went to the court hearing or not, if he rescheduled the court date, and other legal information. None of this information is even verified. The client then has to call the court and find out himself if the ticket has been dismissed or postponed. I believe a Blockchain application with a clear and simple GUI can help change all this. This can change the way we document legal information, save data, and secure trust. It isn't just the client who is having problems, but the lawyer himself.
