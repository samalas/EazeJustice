How Blockchain can help improve the system!

Traffic, Personal Injury, Criminal, Family, & Bankrupcy Law

JustEaze changes the interaction between law and its clientele using Blockchain technology and a simple UI. Some of the common law practices used by the public are criminal justice, traffic law, and personal injury. There are many problems within law firms while working with these law practices, such as mistrust, misinformation, no transparency, bad contracts, and loosing track of data. To solve these issues, a Blockchain law application is needed. Blockchain provides transparency and trust between the lawyer and client.
