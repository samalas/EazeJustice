Legal Management Ecosystem

The ability for clients and attorneys to seamlessly interact with each other while saving time and increasing productivity.

Traffic, Personal Injury, Criminal, Family, Estate & Bankruptcy Law

JustEaze changes the interaction between law and its clientele using technology and a simple UI. Built to simplify and streamline access to justice, this utility application increases the public's ability to quickly resolve common legal issues like criminal defense, traffic, personal injury and estate law. Many law firms and their clients suffer from inefficiencies due to a high case intake volume, misinformation, lack of transparency, communication and time, bad contracts, and losing track of data. JustEaze's team of engineers and lawyers built this application to solve these modern justice issues. Our blockchain technology provides further transparency and trust between the client and their lawyer.
